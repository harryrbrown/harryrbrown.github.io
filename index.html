ScreenPrint is a small program written in Visual Studio to replace Windows' Snipping Tool. It adds keyboard shortcuts, multiple clipboards, automatic saving and a colour picker to make taking screenshots more efficient.

Fallen


Fallen is a game for Android, available on the Google Play Store. The aim of the game is to turn a pipe to keep falling for as long as possible. Includes Google Play Games integration, with leaderboard and achievements.

ARM11 Emulator and Assembler


A third term university project, in which I worked with three other students to write in C an ARM11 emulator and assembler, and then write assembler programs to be assembled and deployed on a Raspberry Pi. The project was selected among the best in the year.

CryptoBot


CryptoBot is a Telegram bot that connects to a Kraken exchange account, providing you with updates on the value of your investments, plus price information for various cryptocurrencies. I have written the bot to be easily modified to suit different users' accounts.

Carrotron2


Developed in 24 hours at HackTheMidlands 2017, Carrotron2 is an Arduino-powered robot that produces a radar map of its surroundings using infrared. Uses several frameworks including React, Flask and Socket.IO.
