About Us

What is Save the Children?

Save the Children is an independent non-profit organisation actively involved in helping children in poverty and in need around the world as well as within the UK. It campaigns to improve the quality of children's lives by dealing with issues such as children's rights, poverty and child abuse, often in areas where there are war and conflict.
