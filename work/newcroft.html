Work:
 The New Croft


See it live at thenewcroft.co.uk.

I developed the website for The New Croft, a state-of-the-art community football and leisure complex based in Haverhill, Suffolk. The facility is the home ground for the town's two semi-professional football clubs, as well as playing host to countless youth fixtures and training sessions throughout the week. Aside from football, the hall and catering facilities are used by several clients for fitness, leisure and entertainment purposes.

The day-to-day running of the site has now been handed over to Haverhill Community Sports Association, but I continue to provide assistance when required.
